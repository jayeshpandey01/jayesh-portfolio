About Me

Born in Mumbai in November 2004, I completed my schooling at Faith English High School, followed by 11th and 12th grades at Satish Pradhan Dnyanasadhana College, Thane. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Engineering at PVGCOE, Nashik, with graduation expected in 2026.

I am deeply passionate about AI, space technology, and entrepreneurship. My journey includes being a finalist in the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2024 and SIH 2023, securing 1st place in an AI competition, and placing 8th at the NITK Hackathon. I have presented innovative solutions at the Bharatiya Antariksh Hackathon 2024 and contributed to geospatial and lunar research challenges.

I have built over 10+ high-impact projects including AI-powered lunar crater detection, social media sentiment analysis, SAR image colorization, and tumor detection using MonoAI. I also developed a social media platform with an AI friend, and have hands-on experience with transformers, Google Gemini, and quantum computing-based financial fraud detection through the “Quantum Detective” project.

My startup journey includes founding Btechnotes, which reached over 100k users in six months. I’ve interned as a Machine Learning Engineer at CIT Internship Studio and participated in multiple ISRO-IIRS certified programs on AI in agriculture, geospatial data, and disaster risk reduction.

My technical skills span Python, C++, SQL, MongoDB, TensorFlow, PyTorch, ReactJS, FastAPI, LLaMA 2, and Hugging Face. I continue exploring emerging technologies with a commitment to innovation, currently authoring a book titled "Life is All About 7 Minutes", inspired by spiritual philosophy. I aspire to make a lasting impact in space-tech, AI research, and the future of education.
